Buck et Al are the product of febrile genius and latent megalomaniac, Buck Shlegeris; a rock opera of love and life as a computer science student, with undergraduate humour, thrilling harmonies, barely concealed arrogance, clever arrangements and some very brightly coloured hair. Add a generous dollop of Zappaesque songwriting freneticism and tight tight pants and you have Buck et Al.

Nigel McRae

Finally we had Buck Et Al (or as I prefer to think of them "Michael Cera The Musical")

Catchy beats and funny self-deprecating lyrics had the crowd laughing and clapping merrily along. They're very new and rather good.

John Griffiths
